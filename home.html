About Us

We are team 1511X Nexus from Arizona and we are currently competting in High Stakes. Our team started back in 2017 and since then our team has gone through multiple rebrandings. Over the years we have been know has 98989Z Llama Bots, 97385A Ragnarok, 1511X Phantoms, 1511H Thonking, and currently 1511X Nexus. This year our team consist of six members, Carson, Griffin, Ethan, Sam, Reed, and Dominic.

Our Current Bot

This is our early season robot and we are currently in the works of a full rebuild. We run 3.25 inch wheels at 360rpms with six motors making us very strong bot. We then run a two motor intake to insure we can load rings quickly and efficently. Our bot then has a two piston mobile goal clamp on the back of the robot that has served us very well this season. Our current bot is very simple but it's also extremly reliable. One of the more unquie features of our bot is our hammer system which allows us scoop the rings out of the corner but also to flip the goals over and then back up, this can be very helpfull in protecting our spare goals from the negitive corner.

Awards

This year we have been to three tournaments so far and plan on going to more.
