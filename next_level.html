Our Team

This year was really where our team started. Team Elements and really all the rest of our IQ years was made up by Carson, Ethan, and Griffin. We didn't know it when we started going into this season but, this season was our most sussfull season we have ever had in all of robotics.

Our Bot

While in all prevoius year we had built the vex IQ hero bot or the Vex IQ claw bot, this year we built our own bot all by ourselves. This was the first time we ever tried to come up with our own design, but we were extemely successfull. It was simple but very competitive in Arizona. We had a claw that could be used to stack and reach the yellow hubs, and we had two "mantis arms" which allowed us to drag up to four hubs across the feild at a time.

The Season

This season was our best by far. By the end of it we ended up wining 13 awards in Arizona which is still our top record for most awards in a season. We were extemely competitive and managed to win many tournaments. We ended the season by making it to the World Championship in Kentucky where we placed 13th in our divison.
